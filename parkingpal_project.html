Parking Pal Mobile Application

Problem

Navigating oneself through a city is faster than its ever been, but there are still many inefficiencies. Reducing these inefficiencies can save time, money, and even lives. Parking is one of the key problems in transportation, and Parking Pal seeks to help make that aspect of travel more efficient.
