About Me


Hi! My name is Oliver and I am a 22 year old Software Engineer, recently graduated from the University of California, San Diego. My focus at this stage of my career is mainly on web development; learning and crafting my skills in both front-end and back-end design so that I can make useful applications that can benefit society. I am especially interested in web development because since the web has become so ubiquitous, the potential number of people web applications can affect far surpasses apps that have more limited scopes.

However, my overall goal is to eventually start working in the autonomous car industry. Self-driving vehicles are a reality just on the horizon and it will have substantial effects on the way society operates. I want to be able to contribute to what will perhaps be one of the most revolutionary technological advancements in our lifetime.

Projects


Cruz Roja Ambulance Web Application

The goal of this project was to provide real-time ambulance tracking to Cruz Roja Tijuana - the city's Red Cross which only has 13 working ambulances. Employing the use of the Django Framework and Leaflet library, I implemented the front-end functionality of creating the webpage and showing current ambulance locations.

Parking Pal

A mobile web application with the objective of reducing the inefficiencies of urban transportation and making parking easier to find. Our application allows people with available spots to rent it out through the app. Users in need of parking can then search the area for spots and purchase the one they want.

Google Chrome Extension - Hello World

This chrome extension greets users with a new wallpaper on the creation of every new tab opening. Each wallpaper is based on a different country and contains facts about each one, educating users about the world with each new tab.
